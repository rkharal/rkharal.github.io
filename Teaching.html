Teaching Philosophy

I strongly believe in teaching from the ground up to ensure students share a solid, consistent foundation. My goal is to minimize gaps in students' understanding that can compound as the material becomes more advanced. I emphasize first principles, such that students can build confidently from fundamentals to complex systems. This approach has been effective across large undergraduate courses and smaller graduate seminars and lectures. I bring a teaching mindset to all speaking engagements: my objective is clear, efficient dissemination of information so that participants leave with a strong grasp of the concepts and how to apply them.
